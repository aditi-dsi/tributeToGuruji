Sant Rampal Ji Maharaj

A man who is saving billions of lives

Here's a time line of Sant Rampal Ji Maharaj

  • 1951 - Born in Dhanana Village, Haryana
  • 1974 - Completed their diploma in Engineering from GBN Govt Polytechnic Nilokheri and became J.E at Irrigation department of Haryana Govt.
  • 1988 - Took initiation from Ramdevanandji
  • 1994 - Ramdevanand Ji permitted them to give name initiation as a Guru
  • 1995 - Resigned from their job to serve the community as their full time
  • 1995 to present - Still serving the community to make people aware about the reality of God and making their lives better
